Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

Clipper is a personal automation tool operated by and for a single individual. It is not a public service and does not collect, process, or store data belonging to any user other than its own operator.

What is accessed

Clipper connects to social media accounts (such as TikTok) that belong to its operator, using each platform's official OAuth authorization flow. Access tokens obtained through this flow are stored locally on the operator's own device and are used only to publish content the operator has chosen to publish to their own account.

What is not done

Clipper does not access any other TikTok user's account, content, or data. It does not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties. It does not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than publishing the operator's own content to the operator's own account.

Data retention

Access tokens and locally processed video/transcript data are retained only on the operator's own device for as long as needed to operate the tool, and can be deleted by the operator at any time.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the operator at the email associated with this account.
